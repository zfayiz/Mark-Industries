About Us




OUR HISTORY ▾

Mark Industries started its operations in 1996, with the production of quality writing tools in collaboration with a well-recognized German manufacturing company. After many years of research we started producing lead pencils in 2009. Now we specialize in wooden pencils specially black lead, colour lead pencils.



OUR MISSION ▾

Our mission is to make a positive difference in the writing instruments (Stationery) field, primarily through the highest standards of quality at affordable price range for its customers/consumers. We always keep an eye on the available latest research & surveys in the world through our R&D department.
